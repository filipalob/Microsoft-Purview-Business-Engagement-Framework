# Data Products and Business Glossary

## Business Glossary

Purpose:

Create common language across the organization.

Examples:

| Term | Definition |
|--------|-----------|
| Customer | Entity receiving services |
| Revenue | Recognized income |

## Data Products

A Data Product combines:

- Data
- Metadata
- Ownership
- SLA
- Quality
