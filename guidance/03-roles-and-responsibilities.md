# Roles and Responsibilities

## Executive Sponsor

Responsible for:

- Governance Funding
- Governance Strategy
- Escalation Decisions

## Data Owner

Responsible for:

- Domain Ownership
- Approvals
- Accountability

## Data Steward

Responsible for:

- Metadata
- Data Quality
- Glossary
- Governance Operations

## Data Custodian

Responsible for:

- Platform Administration
- Security
- Data Integration

## Business SME

Responsible for:

- Business Definitions
- Rule Definition
- Validation
