# Governance Maturity Roadmap

## Level 1 - Initial

Characteristics:

- No Ownership
- No Glossary
- Manual Processes

---

## Level 2 - Managed

Characteristics:

- Governance Domains
- Owners Assigned
- Basic Stewardship

---

## Level 3 - Operational

Characteristics:

- Data Products
- Quality Monitoring
- Governance Metrics

---

## Level 4 - Optimized

Characteristics:

- Automation
- Self-Service Governance
- Enterprise Adoption

---

## Level 5 - AI Ready

Characteristics:

- Trusted Data Products
- Governance by Design
- AI Governance
- Enterprise Metadata Strategy
