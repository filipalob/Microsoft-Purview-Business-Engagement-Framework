# Business Engagement Playbook

## Goal

Create sustainable business participation in governance.

---

## Workshop 1

### Understand Pain Points

Questions:

- Which reports are not trusted?
- Which metrics are disputed?
- Which processes require manual correction?

---

## Workshop 2

### Identify Critical Data Elements

Examples:

- Customer
- Employee
- Product
- Contract

---

## Workshop 3

### Define Business Rules

Example:

Customer Email Must Be Populated

Business Impact:

Customer communication failure.

---

## Workshop 4

### Review Outcomes

Share:

- Data Quality Scores
- Data Product Status
- Stewardship Metrics
