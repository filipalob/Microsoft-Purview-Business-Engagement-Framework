# Why Business Engagement Matters

## Overview

Many organizations deploy governance technologies before establishing governance ownership.

While platforms such as Microsoft Purview provide cataloging, lineage, business glossary, data products, and data quality capabilities, they do not automatically create governance maturity.

Successful governance programs require:

- Business ownership
- Data stewardship
- Accountability
- Governance processes
- Data quality management

Technology enables governance.
Business ownership sustains governance.

---

## Why Governance Programs Fail

The most common failure patterns include:

- Governance treated as an IT initiative
- Lack of Data Owners
- Lack of business participation
- No business glossary
- Undefined stewardship processes
- Focus on tooling rather than outcomes

---

## Guiding Principle

> Governance succeeds when business stakeholders define meaning, ownership, and quality expectations while governance teams operationalize those requirements.
