# Governance Operating Model

## Three Layer Model

### Business Layer

Defines:

- Ownership
- Business Rules
- Data Products
- Quality Expectations

### Governance Layer

Defines:

- Stewardship
- Policies
- Governance Processes
- Compliance Controls

### Technology Layer

Executes:

- Cataloging
- Lineage
- Classification
- Data Quality

---

## Governance Workflow

Business Need

↓

Governance Definition

↓

Technical Implementation

↓

Monitoring

↓

Business Validation

---

## Principles

1. Business owns meaning
2. Governance owns processes
3. Technology enables execution
