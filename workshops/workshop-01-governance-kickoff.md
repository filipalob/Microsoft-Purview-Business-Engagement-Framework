# Governance Kickoff Workshop

## Objective

Establish governance vision, ownership, and business objectives.

---

## Participants

- Executive Sponsor
- Domain Owners
- Governance Office
- Architects
- Data Stewards

---

## Agenda

### 1. Governance Vision

- Why governance?
- Success criteria

### 2. Business Drivers

- Compliance
- AI readiness
- Trusted reporting

### 3. Governance Domains

Define proposed domains.

### 4. Ownership Model

Define owners and stewards.

### 5. Next Steps

Create action plan.
