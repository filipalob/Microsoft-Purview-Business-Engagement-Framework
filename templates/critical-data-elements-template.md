# Critical Data Elements (CDE) Register

## Overview

Critical Data Elements are business-critical pieces of information that require governance oversight.

---

| CDE | Business Domain | Owner | Steward | Business Impact | Regulatory Impact | Priority |
|----------|----------|----------|----------|----------|----------|----------|
